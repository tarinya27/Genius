    About Us

    Our Business was established twenty seven years ago and today the Company has grown steadily, providing state of the art solutions to our valued clients and guiding them in the field of Information Technology enabling them to stand tall among their competitors. Our mission does not end just there. It is the Company’s policy to look after the clients providing the best possible after sales services and maintenance of their IT equipment. This commitment towards after sales service provided the way to be an ISO 9001:2008 accredited Company for After Sales Services and Maintenance of Computers and Accessories.

    We directly import Computer Accessories from reputed manufacturers on regular basis & these accessories are assembled at our Company by Qualified & Experienced Engineers &Technicians, under the Brand name DLC. Today, our own brand “DLC” Computers comes to you with the ISO 9001:2008 International Quality Standards.

    We are also an authorized reseller of world renowned products such as IBM, Intel, HP Compaq, PROLINK, EPSON and GE etc. Appointment of Genius as a Lenovo Business Partner is an endorsement given by the World’s leading PC manufacturer “Lenovo” (formerly IBM PC division) for its commitment towards quality and reliable IT Solutions.

    Further, we have expertise in providing automated total solutions including system integration, development of customized software, Networking, IT Consultancy etc.

    We can with Confidence claim that many reputed Companies; Government Departments & Universities are our regular customers, which we believe is a credit to our Reliable & Prompt after sales Service.
